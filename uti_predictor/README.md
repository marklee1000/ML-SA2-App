UTI Predictor for a local clinic in Northern Mindanao, Philippines. Training data were obtained from the same location and collected from a period of April 2020 to January 2023. It includes and will require demographic information and laboratory findings, specifically:

> Age – age of the patient

> Gender – gender of the patient (male or female)

> Color – color of urine (yellow, light yellow, dark yellow, or straw)

> Transparency – transparency of urine (clear, slightly hazy, hazy, turbid)

> Glucose – presence of glucose in the urine

> Protein – presence of protein in the urine

> pH – acidity or alkalinity of urine

> Specific Gravity – concentration of particles in urine compared to water

> WBC – white blood cell count

> RBC – red blood cell count

> Epithelial Cells – cells that line the surfaces of the urinary tract

> Mucous Threads – presence of mucus strands

> Amorphous Urates – uric acid formations in non-crystalline form

> Bacteria – presence of bacteria in the urine

> Diagnosis – UTI diagnosis outcome (negative or positive)
